📖 Reading Adventures!

Welcome to the magical world of reading! 🌟 Every book is a new adventure, filled with characters, places, and surprises!

🐉 Why Reading is Awesome:

  • 🚀 Travel to space or magical kingdoms — without leaving your room!
  • 🧙‍♂️ Meet fun characters like dragons, wizards, and talking animals!
  • 🧠 Grow your brain with new words and big ideas!

📚 Fun Reading Game:

Match the Word to the Picture:

  • 🐱 Cat – 🟥 Dog 🟩 Cat 🟦 Fish
  • 🍎 Apple – 🟥 Banana 🟩 Apple 🟦 Carrot
  • 🚗 Car – 🟥 Bike 🟩 Car 🟦 Train

🎉 Reading Challenge:

Can you read this short story?

🐰 “Bunny hops in the grass. She sees a flower. She smells it. ‘Yum!’ says Bunny.”

👏 Great job reading! Now draw your own bunny and tell a story!

🌈 Tips for Parents:

  • 📕 Read with your child every day — even 10 minutes helps!
  • 🎵 Use silly voices and rhymes to make stories fun!
  • 🔤 Point to the words as you read them together.
