Subtraction


➖ Let’s Learn Subtraction!

Subtraction means taking away — like sharing or losing something. It’s a super useful skill for solving problems in real life! 🧮

🍭 Easy Example:

You have 5 candies 🍬 and eat 2 🍬. How many are left?

5 - 2 = 3 candies left! 🍬🍬🍬

🐻 Try These Mini Puzzles:

  • 3 - 1 = ?
  • 6 - 2 = ?
  • 5 - 4 = ?
  • 9 - 3 = ?

🎯 Game Time!

Pick the right answer:

  • 7 - 2 → 🟥 6 🟩 5 🟦 4
  • 4 - 3 → 🟥 2 🟩 1 🟦 0
  • 8 - 4 → 🟥 3 🟩 4 🟦 5
🐶 Think of subtraction as giving away toys — how many do you have left?

💡 Tip: You can use your fingers, draw pictures, or count backward to help!
