➕ Fun with Addition!

Let’s add numbers and have fun doing it! Addition helps us count, solve problems, and play number games. It’s like a puzzle where numbers come together! 🎉

🍎 Easy Peasy Example:

If you have 2 apples 🍏 and get 3 more 🍎, how many do you have?

2 + 3 = 5 apples! 🍎🍏🍎🍏🍎

🎲 Try These Yourself:

  • 1 + 2 = ?
  • 4 + 1 = ?
  • 5 + 3 = ?
  • 7 + 2 = ?

🧩 Quick Game:

Match the sum to the right number!

  • 3 + 2 → 🟨 5 🟪 4 🟥 6
  • 1 + 6 → 🟨 8 🟪 7 🟥 6
  • 2 + 4 → 🟨 5 🟪 6 🟥 7
🧠 Remember: Addition is just putting things together — like building blocks!

💡 Tip: Use fingers, draw pictures, or count toys to help solve problems!
