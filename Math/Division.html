Division


➗ Divide and Discover!

Division means sharing things equally! Imagine splitting snacks between friends — that’s division! 🍪👧👦

🍪 Fun Example:

You have 6 cookies and 3 friends. How many does each friend get?

6 ÷ 3 = 2 cookies each! 🍪🍪

🎉 Try These:

  • 4 ÷ 2 = ?
  • 8 ÷ 4 = ?
  • 9 ÷ 3 = ?
  • 6 ÷ 2 = ?

🕹️ Quick Game Time:

Choose the correct answer:

  • 10 ÷ 2 → 🟥 3 🟩 5 🟦 4
  • 12 ÷ 3 → 🟥 6 🟩 3 🟦 4
  • 8 ÷ 2 → 🟥 2 🟩 4 🟦 6
🐻 Think of division like passing out toys – make sure everyone gets the same amount!

💡 Helpful Hint: You can use drawings, fingers, or even real snacks to learn division!
