Multiplication


✖️ Multiply the Fun!

Multiplication is just fast adding! Instead of adding the same number again and again, we multiply! 🧠💥

🍕 Easy Example:

You have 3 plates with 2 slices of pizza 🍕 each. How many slices in total?

3 x 2 = 6 slices of pizza! 🍕🍕🍕🍕🍕🍕

🎲 Try These:

  • 2 x 3 = ?
  • 4 x 2 = ?
  • 5 x 1 = ?
  • 3 x 3 = ?

🏆 Quick Quiz Game:

Pick the correct answer:

  • 2 x 4 → 🟥 6 🟩 8 🟦 10
  • 3 x 3 → 🟥 9 🟩 6 🟦 12
  • 5 x 2 → 🟥 10 🟩 7 🟦 12
🧸 Tip: Multiplication is like groups — how many in total when you have many of the same thing?

💡 Trick: Use skip counting! For 3 x 2, count “3, 6” — that’s your answer!
