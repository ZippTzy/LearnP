What is LearnPlay?

A fun, engaging, and educational platform designed to support primary school students in developing essential academic skills through interactive learning.

What is the website for?

To provide an interactive e-learning platform for primary school students that strengthens their academic skills in reading, writing, and math through fun and engaging activities.

Inspiration behind this idea:

The increasing early exposure of young children to technology, often used for entertainment rather than education, inspired us to create a tool that channels technology into a positive, educational experience.

What problem does it solve or what opportunity does it take advantage of?

It addresses the lack of child-focused educational content in digital media by offering a platform that promotes healthy technology use, academic development, and interactive learning at an early age.


Features:

  • Interactive lessons in reading, writing, and math.

  • Educational games and quizzes to reinforce learning.

  • Progress tracking for students, teachers, and parents.

  • Child-friendly navigation and visual design.
