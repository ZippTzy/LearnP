Astronomy


🪐 Our Solar System


  • ☀️ Sun – The big bright star at the center!
  • 🟤 Mercury – Small and fast!
  • 🟠 Venus – Super hot and cloudy!
  • 🌍 Earth – Our home planet!
  • 🔴 Mars – The red planet with dusty storms!
  • 🟡 Jupiter – The biggest one with a big storm!
  • 🟣 Saturn – It has amazing rings!
  • 🔵 Uranus – Spins sideways!
  • 💙 Neptune – Super windy and far away!

🌌 Cool Space Facts

  • There are more stars in space than grains of sand on Earth!
  • One day on Venus is longer than a whole year there!
  • Jupiter has 95 moons (and counting!).
  • Space is completely silent — no sound at all!
