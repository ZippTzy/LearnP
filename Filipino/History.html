PH Let's Learn: The History of the Philippines!

Hi, Kids! 👋 Ready to take a time-traveling adventure?

⛵ Long, Long Ago...

The Philippines was home to many early people who fished, farmed, and lived in tribes. They made tools from stones and lived close to nature. 🏝️

⚔️ Spanish Arrival (1521)

A man named Ferdinand Magellan came from Spain. That’s when the Spanish started living here too and brought new food, clothes, and religion. ⛪

💪 Filipino Heroes

Many brave Filipinos stood up to fight for freedom. Have you heard of Jose Rizal and Andres Bonifacio? They're our superheroes! 🦸‍♂️🦸‍♀️

🎉 Independence!

After hundreds of years, Filipinos gained freedom on June 12, 1898. That’s why we celebrate Independence Day every year! 🎇🇵🇭

🇺🇸 American Time & 🇯🇵 Japanese Time

The Philippines was also influenced by the USA (they brought schools!) and Japan (during WWII), but we stayed strong and proud. 💼📚

🕊️ Today

Now, the Philippines is a free and happy country with 7,641 islands, rich culture, and friendly people like you! 🏖️👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

📜 “Know your history, and be proud of who you are!”
