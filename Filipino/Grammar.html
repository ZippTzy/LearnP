📚 Let's Learn Grammar – Filipino Style!

Grammar helps us speak and write the right way. Let’s explore the basics in a fun way!

🧩 Parts of Speech in Filipino

  • 🌟 Pangngalan (Nouns) – Names of people, places, or things
    Example: bata (child), paaralan (school), aso (dog)
  • 🔧 Pandiwa (Verbs) – Action words
    Example: tumatakbo (running), kumakain (eating)
  • 🎨 Pang-uri (Adjectives) – Describes nouns
    Example: maganda (beautiful), maliit (small)
  • 🔢 Pang-abay (Adverbs) – Describes actions
    Example: mabilis (fast), dahan-dahan (slowly)

✍️ Sentence Building in Filipino

Simple Sentence:
Ang aso ay tumatakbo. (The dog is running.) 🐶💨

Colorful Sentence:
Ang masayang bata ay mabilis na tumatakbo sa paaralan. (The happy child is quickly running to school.) 🏃‍♂️📚
