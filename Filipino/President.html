Presidents


🏛️ Meet Some Presidents of the Philippines!

Presidents are the leaders of our country. Let’s get to know some of them!

👴 Emilio Aguinaldo – The First President

He became president when the Philippines first declared independence in 1898. He was just 29 years old! 🎉🇵🇭

👓 Manuel L. Quezon – The “Father of the National Language”

He helped make Filipino our national language. He also became the first president of the Commonwealth. 🗣️📘

🌾 Ramon Magsaysay – The President of the People

He was loved by many for being honest and helping the poor. He rode in a jeep to visit everyone! 🚙❤️

💡 Corazon Aquino – The First Woman President

She helped bring back democracy after years of dictatorship. She wore yellow and gave hope to the people! 💛🕊️

📱 Recent Presidents

  • Benigno “Noynoy” Aquino III – Helped improve schools and roads. 🏫🛣️
  • Rodrigo Duterte – Focused on peace and order. 🚓⚖️
  • Ferdinand "Bongbong" Marcos Jr. – Our current leader (as of 2025)! 🇵🇭👨‍💼
🧠 “Being a leader means caring for others and helping your country grow.”
